About

Pucauto is a Pucatrade bot that will automatically accept trades for you.

It works by interacting with a web browser just like you would do. But it's a bot, so it can check for any available trades every five seconds, all day, every day.

Here are some of my results after a few days of running the bot. I was able to get rid of mediocre hard-to-move Theros block cards and foil rares. Normally these are difficult to trade via Pucatrade because supply is high and demand is low, but since Pucauto checks for trades so dilligently I was able to score the trade before other users.
