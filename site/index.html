About

Pucauto is a Pucatrade bot that will automatically accept trades for you.

It works by interacting with a web browser just like you would do. But it's a bot, so it can check for any available trades every five seconds, all day, every day, until you run out of cards to trade... which is what happened to me when I built it and used it for a week. I finally got rid of all of my old Theros block cards and random foils that folks at card shops aren't interested in.
